What is Artificial Intelligence?

Understanding Artificial Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a field of computer science that creates machines able to perform tasks that normally need human intelligence, such as learning, understanding language, and solving problems. It uses data and algorithms to recognize patterns and make smart decisions.

More Detail

Machine Learning

Systems that learn and improve from experience without being explicitly programmed.

Neural Networks

Computing systems inspired by biological neural networks in the human brain.

Deep Learning

Subset of ML using layered neural networks to analyze complex patterns.

Natural Language

Enabling computers to understand, interpret, and generate human language.

Applications of Artificial Intelligence

AI in Healthcare

AI in healthcare improves diagnosis, predicts diseases, automates workflows, analyzes medical images, and helps doctors deliver faster, more accurate, and personalized patient care.

AI in healthcare Medical AI Healthcare automation AI diagnosis

AI in Finance

AI in finance detects fraud, automates risk analysis, enhances customer service, improves trading decisions, and helps banks offer smarter, data-driven financial solutions.

AI in Entertainment
